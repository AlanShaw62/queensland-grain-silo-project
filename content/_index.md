# \# Queensland Grain Silo Project

# 

# Welcome. This site is the culmination of a personal photography project to record the wheat silos of the former Queensland State Wheat Board and its successors. Over the past ten years, I've visited all of the Board's former grain facilities, some still in use, some still standing but out of use and some that are no longer standing. Each location has its own gallery of photos on this site, along with notes and background information.
